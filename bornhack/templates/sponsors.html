Sponsoring

We are looking for sponsors. If you work for a company that you believe might be able to sponsor BornHack please direct the right people to this page.

For sponsor inquiries, please reach out to sponsors@bornhack.dk

A sponsorship can be in the range of DKK 5000,- and up. Your sponsorship could, for example, be used for:

  • Bar area
  • Lounge area
  • BBQ (coal and renting of grills)
  • T-shirt's
  • Shuttle bus to and from the venue on the first and the last camping day

Please reach out to us on sponsors@bornhack.dk if you have a creative idea that you would be interested in sponsoring :-)
